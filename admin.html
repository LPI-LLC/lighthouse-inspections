04 Competitive Landscape

Small Towns = Wide Open

Towns like Centreville, Constantine, Colon, Dowagiac, Cassopolis, and Edwardsburg have near-zero SEO competition for home inspection searches. A single well-optimized page can rank on page 1 quickly.

Kalamazoo = Volume Play

The Kalamazoo-Portage metro has 9+ competing inspectors doing SEO. But search volume is significantly higher. Strategy: win the small towns first to build domain authority, then compete in Kalamazoo from a stronger position.

US-131 Corridor

Three Rivers → Schoolcraft → Vicksburg → Kalamazoo/Portage. This highway is your natural service line and should be a marketing emphasis.
