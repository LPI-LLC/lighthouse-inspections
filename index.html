Credentials

Certified & Qualified

Extensive training and certification through InterNACHI® — the world's largest association of home inspectors.

Residential Property Inspector

Comprehensive training in home system inspection

Covers all major home systems and components — ensuring a thorough evaluation of the property you're buying or selling.

Plumbing Inspector

Expert evaluation of residential plumbing systems

Specialized training in water supply, drain/waste/vent systems, fixtures, and water heaters. Catches issues that lead to costly water damage and mold.

Attic, Insulation & Ventilation

Evaluates attic conditions, insulation, and airflow

Improper insulation and ventilation are among the most common causes of energy waste, ice dams, and premature roof deterioration.

Moisture Intrusion

Detects hidden water damage and leak sources

Undetected moisture leads to mold growth, wood rot, structural damage, and unhealthy indoor air quality.

Exterior Inspector

Focused on siding, grading, and exterior components

Identifies improper drainage, deteriorating siding, and flashing failures that can lead to water intrusion and structural damage.

Roof Inspector

Trained to detect roof defects and damage

Identifies problems early, helps you understand remaining roof life, and documents damage for insurance claims.

Chimney Inspector

Specialized in fireplace, stove, and chimney safety

Chimney and fireplace defects are a leading cause of house fires — a certified inspector knows exactly what to look for.

Deck Inspector

Specialized in deck safety and structural integrity

Deck collapses cause thousands of injuries annually — proper inspection of structural connections and load-bearing components is critical.

WDO Inspector

Identifies termites, beetles, and wood-destroying fungi

Wood-destroying organisms cause severe structural damage often hidden from plain sight — early detection can save tens of thousands in repairs.

Infrared / Thermography

Uses thermal imaging to reveal hidden problems

Reveals hidden moisture intrusion, missing insulation, electrical hot spots, and air leakage invisible to the naked eye.

New Construction

Certified across all twelve inspection disciplines

New homes can have just as many defects as existing ones — builder mistakes and code violations are common, and this certification covers every major system.

11th Month Warranty

Protects your builder warranty before it expires

Identifies defects before your one-year builder warranty runs out, so repairs are at the builder's expense.

Annual Home Maintenance

Routine yearly inspections to protect your investment

Catches small problems before they become expensive repairs and helps prioritize your maintenance spending.
