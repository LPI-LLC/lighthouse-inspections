What We Offer

Inspection Services

Every inspection follows InterNACHI® Standards of Practice, delivered with clear communication and detailed digital reports.

Residential Home Inspections

Comprehensive evaluation of structure, electrical, plumbing, roofing, and mechanical systems. We leave no stone unturned so you can buy with confidence.

WDO Inspections

Thorough inspection for wood-destroying organisms — identifying activity, damage, and conducive conditions that could compromise your property's integrity.

Thermal Imaging

Infrared technology that reveals hidden moisture intrusion, insulation deficiencies, and energy loss invisible to the naked eye.

Chimney, Deck & Roof

Specialized inspections of exterior structures — evaluating chimneys, decking, and roofing systems for safety, function, and general condition.

Plumbing, Electrical & HVAC

In-depth evaluation of your home's critical systems — water supply, drainage, wiring, panels, heating, and cooling. Safety and efficiency assessed.

Detailed Digital Reports

Clear, thorough reporting with high-resolution photos, findings organized by system, and practical recommendations delivered digitally.
