Inspections That Understand Dowagiac

Dowagiac (pop. ~5,700) is Cass County's largest city, situated between Indian Lake, Dewey Lake, and other popular waterways. Many properties in the area have lake access or are set on the rolling terrain typical of this part of Michigan.

Older homes near downtown often feature original hardwood, plaster walls, and stone or block foundations that need careful evaluation. Lake-adjacent properties bring additional considerations — moisture management, grading and drainage, crawl space conditions, and dock or seawall structures.

Lighthouse's thermal imaging capability is especially useful in Dowagiac, where hidden moisture from high water tables can go undetected for years.
