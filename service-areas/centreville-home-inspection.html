Local Inspections for Centreville Homes

Centreville (pop. ~1,300) is a quiet, historic village that serves as the St. Joseph County seat. The housing stock includes many older homes dating to the late 1800s and early 1900s, many with architectural character — wide porches, original woodwork, and established lots.

These homes often come with issues typical of their era: aging electrical systems, outdated plumbing, foundation settling, and roof wear. The surrounding rural areas feature farmhouses and properties on larger lots where outbuildings, well systems, and septic need attention.

Being based in nearby Three Rivers means we can often schedule Centreville inspections on short notice.
