Home Inspections for Constantine Properties

Constantine (pop. ~1,900) is a charming village along the St. Joseph River, just a short drive south of Three Rivers. The village has a mix of older homes with character — many dating to the early 1900s — alongside newer rural properties on the outskirts.

River-adjacent homes require particular attention to grading, drainage, and basement moisture. Older village homes commonly present with original wiring upgrades needed, aging roofing materials, and foundation issues from decades of Michigan freeze-thaw cycles.

As a neighbor to Constantine, Lighthouse understands the building patterns here and can often accommodate same-day or next-day scheduling for inspections in the area.
