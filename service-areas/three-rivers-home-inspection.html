Free Post-Storm Property Assessments

If your home was affected by the storm, Lighthouse is offering complimentary property assessments to help you understand the condition of your home. Here's what we'll check:

  • Structural integrity — foundation cracks, shifted walls, compromised framing
  • Roof and exterior damage — missing shingles, soffit and fascia damage, siding impact
  • Water intrusion — moisture from breached roofing, broken windows, or compromised seals
  • Electrical and mechanical systems — downed lines, damaged panels, HVAC displacement
  • Tree and debris impact — damage from fallen trees to structures, fencing, and utilities

Local Knowledge

Why Three Rivers Trusts Lighthouse

Lighthouse Property Inspections is headquartered right here in Three Rivers — this is our home. We know the neighborhoods, the builders, and the unique characteristics of the local housing stock. Three Rivers sits at the confluence of the St. Joseph, Rocky, and Portage rivers, and that proximity to water means we pay special attention to moisture intrusion, grading, and drainage around every property we inspect.

Much of Three Rivers' charm comes from its pre-war craftsman-style homes and mid-century bungalows that line the streets near downtown and along the river. These homes have character, but they also come with common findings we see regularly: aging foundations with mortar deterioration, outdated electrical panels, original plumbing that may be nearing the end of its service life, and roofing systems that take a beating from harsh Michigan winters. We know exactly what to look for because we inspect these homes year-round.

Newer construction on the outskirts of town brings its own set of considerations — from verifying proper grading and drainage to ensuring builders followed current code requirements. Whether you're buying a historic home on Portage Avenue or a new build off Broadway Road, Lighthouse delivers the same thorough, standards-based inspection with a same-day digital report so you can make informed decisions quickly.
