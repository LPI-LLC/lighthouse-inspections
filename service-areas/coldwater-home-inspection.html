Why Coldwater Homebuyers Call Lighthouse

Coldwater (pop. ~14,000) is Branch County's largest city, known for its historic downtown, active lake community, and affordable housing market. Lakefront and near-lake homes require special attention — moisture intrusion, shoreline grading, dock structures, and seasonal freeze-thaw damage are common concerns that many inspectors overlook.

The historic downtown features beautiful older homes with character, but these often come with knob-and-tube wiring, aging foundations, and outdated plumbing. These are not reasons to walk away from a home — but they are things you need to know about before you close.

Lighthouse's thermal imaging capabilities are especially valuable for lake properties where hidden moisture issues can be costly. We bring the same InterNACHI-certified thoroughness to every Coldwater inspection, whether it's a downtown Victorian or a lakefront ranch.
