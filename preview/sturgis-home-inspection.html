Local Knowledge

Home Inspections Tailored for Sturgis

Sturgis is the largest city in St. Joseph County with approximately 11,000 residents, and its housing market reflects a community that has grown steadily over the decades. From the well-established neighborhoods near downtown with their early 1900s homes to the newer developments expanding outward along the US-12 corridor, Sturgis offers a wide range of properties — each with its own inspection considerations.

Older homes in the Sturgis downtown core and surrounding blocks often feature original plaster walls, aging HVAC systems, and foundations that have experienced decades of Michigan freeze-thaw cycles. Foundation settling is one of the most common findings we document in this area, along with outdated electrical systems that may not meet modern safety standards. Properties near Sturgis Lake and the Fawn River present additional considerations, including moisture management, proper grading, and potential water intrusion in lower-level spaces.

Lighthouse Property Inspections is based just a short drive away in Three Rivers, which means we can schedule quickly and arrive promptly for your Sturgis inspection. Our proximity to the area also means we have a deep familiarity with the local building practices, common materials, and regional issues that affect homes throughout St. Joseph County. Every inspection follows InterNACHI Standards of Practice and includes a same-day digital report with high-resolution photos and clear, actionable findings.
