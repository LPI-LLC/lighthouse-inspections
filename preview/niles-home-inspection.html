Local Expertise

Home Inspections for Niles Properties

Niles (pop. ~12,000) is one of Berrien County's larger cities, with deep roots as a river town along the St. Joseph River. The housing stock includes stately older homes from the late 1800s and early 1900s, many with original hardwood, stone foundations, and architectural character — but also common issues like outdated electrical, aging roofing, and potential moisture concerns in basements near the river.

The neighborhoods south of downtown include more mid-century construction with their own typical findings — aluminum wiring, original windows, and aging mechanical systems. Every era of construction has its strengths and its watch-outs.

Lighthouse understands the building patterns of river towns and knows where to look for the issues that matter most. Whether you're buying a century home with character or a 1960s ranch, we deliver clear, actionable reports so you can move forward with confidence.
